Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Open the app, tap "Planos de Assinatura" in the menu, choose your plan, and follow the checkout steps.

How do I restore purchases?

Go to "Configurações > Assinaturas" and tap "Restaurar Compras."

Troubleshooting

Unable to fetch recipes

• Check your internet connection.
• Update the app to the latest version.
• If the problem persists, contact support.

Subscription purchase failed

• Make sure your App Store / Play Store account is active.
• Confirm you have the correct product IDs configured.
• Try restoring purchases from the subscription page.
